About the Project

This project uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to classify images of apples and bananas as either fresh or rotten. The model is trained on a dataset of high-quality images, enabling it to accurately distinguish between fresh and rotten fruits. The system is designed to help users quickly identify the quality of fruits, reducing food waste and ensuring better consumption habits.

How It Works

The CNN model analyzes the visual features of the fruit images, such as color, texture, and shape, to determine their freshness. Users can upload an image of an apple or banana, and the system will predict whether the fruit is fresh or rotten with a confidence score.
